Membership Info & Roster Page

They say membership has its privileges. It does at Team Utopia. Not only do we enjoy ourselves at races, we also train together (optional, of course) and develop a camaraderie that extends far beyond the trails, roads, and track. For $25, NEW 2014 members will receive a team jersey, a monthly training schedule, and waived or reduced entry fee into some races. Renewing members will receive a Team Utopia item of their choosing. Similar to USATF, your paid membership will be valid for the 2014 calendar year.

So, join our team and help us grow! Team Utopia is a competitive club that is open to all interested individuals of any ability. Benefits may change during the year depending on sponsorship, numbers, etc. Additionally, in the past we have received reduced prices on merchandise (t-shirts). As our numbers and sponsorship increase, so will our benefits.

We train together (not a requirement to be a member) several times a week and represent Team Utopia at local and out of area team competitions. We also volunteer at several local races. Team Utopia membership requires a Team Utopia 2014 Membership Application and $25 dues. We encourage all Team Utopia members to join USA Track and Field as well (www.usatf.org). Contact Coach or a club officer for more information.
